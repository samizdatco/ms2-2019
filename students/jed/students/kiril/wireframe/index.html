The analysis employs a multilinear regression to quantify the effect of travel.

There is 1 dependent and 8 independent variables that make up the model:

Dependent variable:

PTW: Ranking points earned this week

Independent variables:

ALW: Age of the player last week
MLW: Number of matches played last week
OLW: Average rank of opponents last week
Tournament category last week (expressed in dummy variables)
TTW: Miles traveled between last week's and this week's tourament
MTW: Number of matches played this week
OTW: Average rank of opponents this week
Tournament category this week (expressed in dummy variables)

Data Source: WTA records for full season 2018 for the year-end top 30 women's tennis players. Full research paper with citations available here.
